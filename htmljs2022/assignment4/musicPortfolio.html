Waltz

This is the most recent song I've written. It uses a descending chord progression. It is in a minor key, yet most of the chords are actually major. I really like the melody to this one, to me it feels like a lullaby. Also, despite it's length, I still feel this song should have been a bit longer.

Paper

This is a slower song which was mainly based off the middle section of another song I wrote. The file is over 3 minutes, but that is only because it loops the song. Out of the other songs here, this is the oldest.

Descend

This song is relativley recent. It mainly sticks to the same descending chord progression which I really like. I think this song has a similar feeling to the next song on this page, slightly chaotic.

Carnival

This song is relativley quick and a bit chaotic. I'm most proud of the bass piano part and the slightly unstable feeling I managed to generate. The making of this song was interesting, as I had trouble understanding the key signature and chord progressions due to the many accidentals.
